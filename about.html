About Me

Most of us would agree that good health and good education are powerful nourishment as we grow the good versions of ourselves into the even better versions we envision. My better self is someone who can meld software engineering and data science to create tools that make good health and good education more accessible to everyone.

My latest milestone towards becoming that better self was graduating from Grace Hopper Academy, a coding bootcamp that specializes in fullstack JavaScript. I'm currently looking for a junior developer role, preferably one that exposes me to data science and machine learning. But what matters most to me is fit. I will choose people over product any day of the week because, in my experience, harder-working teams with aligned values progress further than more talented teams with great ideas.

So what are my values? Assuming the best intentions, giving the best effort, wondering why, asking why not, figuring out how, failing well, changing my mind, lending a hand, riding my bike, and laughing honestly.

Oh! And french fries. I deeply appreciate french fries. 🍟

About This Site

I initially built this site using Yahoo's Pure, but then decided it was more than I needed. So I built it again from scratch with HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript.

The first-choice fonts for this site are Shrikhand for header text, Montserrat for headings, Roboto Slab for paragraphs, and Poppins for everything else. The icons are from Font Awesome.

Other Things I've Made

PAM Lab Logo

If the letters look a little unusual, it's because they're symbols from the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA). PAM stands for Phonetics, Acquisition and Multilingualism. The IPA characters and the abstracted sound waves represent the phonetics and acquisition parts. The different color fills represent the multilingualism part. The next time you're wondering about cross-linguistic phonological similarity, check out their Twitter.

Personal Website Logo
